Inaugural Conference

26 to 28 April 2021

The Privacy Studies Journal is an interdisciplinary, open access, peer-reviewed journal published by the Danish National Research Foundation Centre for Privacy Studies, University of Copenhagen.

At its inaugural conference, we will have a series of presentations by eminent specialists on privacy from different fields and strands of research. It is our ambition that the atmosphere of the workshop is one of generosity, experimentation, and mutual inspiration,
even though we are not in the same room.
